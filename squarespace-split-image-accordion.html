So often, we are told our posture is "bad" or "wrong." I view it differently: your body has simply found clever ways to keep you moving. A postural assessment is our chance to pinpoint where your body exists in space, and to discover if there are opportunities for optimization. Together, we'll create a personalized plan to help you move more freely, and do the things you love to do!

My training approach is rooted in exercise science, and physiology at its core. Combining my Bachelor's Degree in Exercise Science, experience working as a personal trainer with diverse populations for 8 years, and devotion to continuing education, I translate complex health concepts into a simple effective roadmap for you.
