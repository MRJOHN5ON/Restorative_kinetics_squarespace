Safety First
Never test a true 1RM alone. Use submaximal testing (3-5 reps) to safely predict your max without the risk of total failure.
📏
Accuracy Rule
Form is everything. If your technique breaks down, the rep doesn't count for the calculation. Most accurate under 10 reps.
